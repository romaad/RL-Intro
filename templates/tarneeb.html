How to play Tarneeb
  • 4 players — You & Partner vs two opponents
  • Bidding: starting at 7, each player bids the number of tricks their team will win (or passes). The highest bidder names the trump suit.
  • Playing: leads any card; others must follow suit if possible. Highest card of the led suit wins unless a trump is played.
  • 13 tricks per round. If the bidding team wins ≥ bid tricks, they score all their tricks. Otherwise they score −bid.
  • First team to reach 31 points wins the game.
