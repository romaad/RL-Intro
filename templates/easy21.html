How to play Easy21
  • Cards are numbered 1–10
  • Black cards (2/3 chance) add to your sum
  • Red cards (1/3 chance) subtract from your sum
  • Going below 1 or above 21 = bust (you lose)
  • Press HIT to draw another card
  • Press STICK to end your turn; the dealer then plays
  • Dealer must draw until their sum reaches 17 or higher
  • Highest sum (without busting) wins; ties are a draw
